Stackr Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Stackr is a personal supplement and protocol tracking app. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

Data Storage

All data you enter into Stackr (items, protocols, doses, wellness logs) is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. We do not collect or have access to this data by default.

Optional Account & Sync

If you choose to create an account and enable cloud sync, your stack data is encrypted and stored on our servers solely for the purpose of syncing across your devices. We do not sell, share, or use this data for any other purpose.

Purchases

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not store or have access to your payment information. Purchase status is managed by RevenueCat.

Analytics

We do not use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
